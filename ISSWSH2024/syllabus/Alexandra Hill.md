## ページ 16

Impact of Chemotherapy on Sexual Health
© OncoPelvic PT, LLC 2024
16
Various cancer diagnoses (Reimer 2023)
• Chemotherapy can cause sensory symptoms 
of the genital organs and sexual dysfunction 
by damaging genital organ nerve fibers
• Chemotherapy and hormonal therapy can 
trigger hormonal disruption leading to sexual 
dysfunction  
• CIPN affects hands and feet, impacting use in 
sexual activity 
Image depicts stocking-glove 
distribution of CIPN

Used for tumors that are fueled by 
hormones (estrogen, progesterone, 
or testosterone) to grow 
Drugs or surgical interventions to 
reduce the amount of hormones in 
the body or stop them from acting 
on cancer cells.

Impact of Physical Activity on Sexual Health
© OncoPelvic PT, LLC 2024

• Improves body image 
• Reduces cancer related fatigue

• Lower odds of Hypoactive Sexual
